# WATTLY App Icon Design Specifications

## Icon Concept
Professional electric meter app icon for WATTLY Smart Meter Operations

## Design Elements

### Background
- **Shape**: Rounded square with 22% corner radius
- **Color**: Blue gradient from #1e3a8a to #3b82f6
- **Size**: 1024x1024px (will be scaled down for different uses)

### Main Element: Electric Meter
- **Position**: Center of icon
- **Color**: Light gray/white (#f8fafc to #e2e8f0 gradient)
- **Shape**: Rectangular meter housing with rounded corners
- **Size**: ~60% of icon width, ~70% of icon height

### Digital Display
- **Position**: Upper portion of meter
- **Background**: Dark blue/black (#0f172a to #1e293b gradient)
- **Text**: "88888" in green LED style (#10b981)
- **Unit**: "kWh" below the numbers

### WATTLY Branding
- **Text**: "WATTLY" in bold sans-serif
- **Color**: Dark blue (#1e40af)
- **Position**: Center of meter body
- **Size**: Prominent but not overwhelming

### Technical Details
- **Connection Terminals**: 4 circular terminals at bottom (L1, L2, L3, N)
- **LED Indicator**: Small red LED dot
- **Meter Seal**: Small red seal indicator
- **Electric Symbol**: Small lightning bolt at top

## File Requirements

### Primary Icon (icon.png)
- **Size**: 1024x1024px
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Usage**: iOS app icon, general use

### Adaptive Icon (adaptive-icon.png)
- **Size**: 1024x1024px
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Usage**: Android adaptive icon foreground
- **Note**: Should work with circular, square, and rounded square masks

### Splash Screen (splash.png)
- **Size**: 1284x2778px (iPhone 12 Pro Max resolution)
- **Background**: Blue gradient matching icon
- **Content**: Larger version of WATTLY logo with meter graphic

### Favicon (favicon.png)
- **Size**: 48x48px
- **Format**: PNG
- **Usage**: Web favicon

## Implementation Notes
1. The icon should be recognizable at small sizes (16x16px)
2. Maintain high contrast for accessibility
3. Use consistent branding colors throughout the app
4. Ensure the design works on both light and dark backgrounds
5. The meter design should be modern and professional
6. Include subtle shadows and gradients for depth

## Tools for Creation
- Adobe Illustrator or Figma for vector design
- Export to PNG at required resolutions
- Use online tools like app-icon.co for generating all required sizes
- Test on actual devices to ensure clarity
